Questions traders ask

Short answers, no fluff.

Are the example values real-time?

Our values are maintained and updated regularly by our team based on active Adopt Me trading trends. We don't claim live data, but we ensure accuracy through continuous monitoring of the community marketplace.

Do variants change worth?

Absolutely. Ride, Fly, Neon, and Mega variants significantly impact a pet's trading value. Our platform accounts for these modifiers so you can see exactly what your specific pet configuration is worth in the current market.

How should I use values in negotiation?

Treat our values as a baseline reference point. Real trades depend on demand, personal preference, and timing. Use the data to start fair conversations, not as absolute law—trading is still an art.

Why do some pets spike?

Limited releases, seasonal events, and game updates can create sudden demand surges. When a pet becomes unavailable or gains popularity through YouTuber showcases, its value can jump rapidly. We track these shifts to keep you informed.

What does "verified" mean here?

Verified values come from cross-referencing multiple trusted trading communities, analyzing completed trades, and consulting experienced Adopt Me players. It's our methodology for ensuring the numbers you see reflect real-world consensus.

Can new pets be added?

Yes. We add new pets as soon as they're released in Adopt Me and enough trade data exists to establish accurate values. Typically, this happens within days of a major game update or event.
