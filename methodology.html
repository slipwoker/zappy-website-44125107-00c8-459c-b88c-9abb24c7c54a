The rules behind the numbers

Values are only useful when they are consistent, transparent, and community-aligned.

Consistency over hype

We don't chase temporary spikes or social media trends. Every value is calculated using the same proven methodology, ensuring Adopt Me players can trust the data whether they're checking a legendary dragon or a common dog.

Variant premiums are explicit

Neon, mega, fly, ride—every variant modifier is clearly shown with its own multiplier. No hidden calculations or guesswork. You see exactly how each trait affects the final value.

Ranges over false precision

Trading is fluid. We provide realistic value ranges instead of fake exactness, giving you the context to negotiate confidently without pretending we know the exact worth down to the decimal.

Change logs over surprise shifts

Every value update is logged and timestamped. When the market moves, you'll know why, when, and by how much. No mysterious overnight changes—just transparent, traceable adjustments.
